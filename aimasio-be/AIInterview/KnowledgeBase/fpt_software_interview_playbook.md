# FPT Software Interview Playbook

## Boi canh tuyen dung pho bien
- Du an outsourcing cho khach hang quoc te (Nhat, US, EU).
- Team lam viec theo Agile/Scrum, giao tiep lien team va lien bo phan.
- Uu tien on dinh delivery, chat luong code, va kha nang phoi hop.

## Tieu chi danh gia ung vien ky thuat
- Problem solving trong dieu kien rang buoc ve deadline, budget, quality.
- Ownership: chu dong de xuat giai phap, theo doi den ket qua.
- Collaboration: phoi hop BA, QA, PM, va khach hang.
- Communication: giai thich quyet dinh ky thuat ro rang, de hieu.
- Adaptability: hoc nhanh domain moi va cong nghe moi.

## Kieu cau hoi HR nen dat
- Cau hoi tinh huong gan boi canh du an thuc te.
- Cau hoi dao sau theo trade-off: chi phi, thoi gian, chat luong.
- Cau hoi hanh vi: cach ung xu khi conflict, pressure, requirement mo ho.
- Cau hoi ket qua: co metric, tac dong den team/khach hang.

## Mau dao sau dung cach
- "Trong boi canh du an outsource, em uu tien gi khi khach hang thay doi scope sat deadline?"
- "Neu budget han che nhung yeu cau quality cao, em can bang ra sao?"
- "Em co the neu vi du cu the va ket qua do luong duoc khong?"
