# Outsourcing Japan Delivery Context

## Dac thu khach hang Nhat
- Uu tien tinh on dinh, do tin cay, va quy trinh ro rang.
- Ky luat deadline cao, minh bach tien do, bao cao dinh ky.
- Chuan tai lieu va handover duoc danh gia cao.

## Nhan manh khi phong van
- Kha nang tiep nhan feedback va dieu chinh nhanh.
- Lam ro requirement mo ho truoc khi code.
- Quan ly risk som, de xuat plan B.
- Giao tiep lich su, ngan gon, ro muc tieu va hanh dong tiep theo.

## Trade-off thuong gap
- Budget thap vs quality cao.
- Deadline gap vs technical debt.
- Scope lon vs nguon luc team han che.

## Follow-up nen uu tien
- Hoi ve cach ra quyet dinh khi co xung dot muc tieu.
- Hoi ve cach do luong ket qua va bai hoc sau du an.
- Hoi ve kha nang collaboration voi QA/BA/PM trong tinh huong ap luc.
